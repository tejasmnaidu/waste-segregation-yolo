Deploy an efficient waste detection model to automate litter identification and recycling sorting processes. This project provides a pre-trained computer vision model and a 2.3k-image dataset annotated for bottles, cans, and cartons, offering a robust foundation for smart waste management and robotic cleanup systems.

## Ways to Use YOLO Waste Detection Model

1. **Automated Recycling Sorting:** Integrate the model into material recovery facility (MRF) conveyor systems to automatically sort diverse materials like aluminum cans, tetra packs, and different types of plastics, increasing the purity of recycled streams.
2. **Smart Public Trash Bins:** Power IoT-connected waste bins in urban areas that can identify the type of trash being deposited and provide real-time feedback to users or trigger alerts when specific bins (e.g., paper or plastic) are full.
3. **Circular Economy Analytics:** Deploy the model at waste collection points to gather data on the most common types of disposable packaging used in a region, helping brands and governments design better waste reduction strategies.
4. **Illegal Dumping Detection:** Use the model on mobile patrol vehicles or fixed cameras to identify large-scale discarded items like furniture or electronics in unauthorized areas, facilitating faster cleanup and enforcement.
5. **Industrial Waste Management:** Implement the model in manufacturing plants to monitor and categorize scrap metal, wood, and chemical containers, ensuring that industrial byproducts are disposed of or repurposed according to environmental regulations.
